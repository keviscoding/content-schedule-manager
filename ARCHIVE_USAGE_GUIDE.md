# Archive Feature - Quick Start Guide

## What is the Archive Feature?

The archive feature lets you temporarily hide channels you're not actively working on. Think of it as putting channels "on the shelf" - they're still there, just out of sight until you need them again.

## Why Use It?

- **Keep your dashboard clean**: Focus only on channels you're actively managing
- **Don't lose data**: Unlike deleting, archiving preserves everything
- **Easy to restore**: Bring channels back anytime with one click
- **Better organization**: Separate active projects from paused ones

## Common Use Cases

### Seasonal Channels
Archive channels that are only active during certain times of the year.

### Paused Projects
Archive channels for projects you've temporarily put on hold.

### Testing/Experimental Channels
Archive test channels you might want to reference later.

### Completed Series
Archive channels for completed video series you might revisit.

## Tips

1. **Archive, don't delete**: Unless you're 100% sure you won't need the channel again, archive it instead of deleting
2. **Regular cleanup**: Review your active channels monthly and archive inactive ones
3. **Quick restore**: Archived channels can be restored instantly - no data loss
4. **Editors still have access**: Editors assigned to archived channels can still view them in the archived view
